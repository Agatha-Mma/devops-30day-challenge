## Key Concepts Learned

### DevOps as a Cultural and Operational Shift

DevOps is not just a collection of tools but a practice that emphasizes:

- Automation
- Collaboration between development and operations
- Continuous improvement
- Monitoring and feedback

This aligns closely with cloud computing, where infrastructure must remain scalable, observable, and reliable.

### Importance of Version Control

Git enables:

- Tracking changes over time
- Collaboration across teams
- Safe rollback in case of errors

Version control is foundational to DevOps workflows.
