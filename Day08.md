# Day 8 – DevOps Is About Reducing Surprises

Today I shifted my mindset.

DevOps is not primarily about speed.
It is about reducing surprises in production.

Surprises like:
- Failed deployments
- Environment inconsistencies
- Unexpected downtime

The goal is stability and predictability.

Speed becomes a byproduct of well-designed systems.

Key Insight:
The best DevOps systems are the ones that behave predictably.
